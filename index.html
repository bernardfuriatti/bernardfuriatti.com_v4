About
me

I am a Brazilian in love with sun, cold açaí, creativity and new knowledge. This love for creativity is linked to its power to transform our society's problems into opportunities that provide our own human evolution.

And therefore, I am always seeking to learn new skills and live new experiences to fuel my creativity. I love playing piano, developing new languages like English and Spanish, meditating, and photographing.

In 2016, I started in User Experience and Interface Design. I've been helping large companies in the digital transformation using User-centric, Lean UX, and data-driven approaches to solve business problems.

Besides, I studied and worked in software development, which collaborates to empathize with the technical team. It also helps design new products, especially in hypothesis validations, when it's essential to be validated quickly.

UX Case

Simplifying the process to save lives and the environment

Design Sprint Research Correspondence analysis Usability test Wireframe High Fidelity

In the global mining industry, characterized by a complex and hazardous production chain, lives and the environment are at risk during routine activities. Consequently, the need for information becomes paramount to ensure strategic and effective decisions, safeguarding the well-being of over 76.000 workers and millions of community members around the world.

Other
projects
I've Made

Providing strategic information and streamlining employees' time.

In a complex and billion-dollar operation, information is the most precious resource in decision-making. To prevent this information from getting lost amidst a busy routine, the solution records and informs stakeholders and employees about the most relevant events. As a result, this solution becomes valuable in the daily lives of thousands of employees scattered across different cities and countries.

Saving millions in ocean ore shipping

Defining a strategy for shipping minerals to the other side of the world is a complex task filled with variables. Using algorithms to assist in this decision-making process will streamline the procedure; however, it's still necessary to present the outcomes through an interface that displays the algorithm results to those in charge. This will aid them in reconsidering and reassembling their strategy.

Tracking the origin of leads through the sales funnel until their conversion.

In a multinational company with various customer acquisition channels, it's necessary to register and map thousands of prospects until the sale is actually finalized. The solution streamlines the daily tasks of the salespeople and provides valuable insights for leaders to develop strategies and identify issues in the real estate sales process.
